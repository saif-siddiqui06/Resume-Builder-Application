Tips for Creating an ATS-Friendly Resume

  1. Use a simple, clean layout with clear headings to make your resume easy to read.
  2. Avoid using images, graphics, or charts, as they can confuse ATS software.
  3. Stick to standard fonts like Arial or Times New Roman to ensure compatibility.
  4. Include relevant keywords from the job description to align with the ATS algorithm.
  5. Save your resume in a format like PDF or DOCX that can be easily read by ATS.
